We fired the guy after all. The nerve of this fellow - PM sat on him for a whole day, he produced exactly one boilerplate proposal document, and then he demanded 50% down before even starting on any of the drawings. Which is a perfectly reasonable thing to have done, if it had been 6 weeks ago (around when we first engaged him) and without needing any sitting-on. So he got fired and we engaged a different draftie, who was recommended by the house mover and was indeed responsive. 

Council is being council. In other words, I'm paying 10k for the privilege of producing new drawings of an existing house with existing drawings, so that a guy at council can tick a box saying "we received all these drawings". And by drawings, I mean all possible drawings that anyone could possibly want to see, including renders. 

Powerco is being powerco. Their "6-8 working weeks" to approve the new connection has come and gone, and all they're saying is that it's "in their workflow". 

In other news, I'm not getting either of my top options for project 2. The seller wasn't selling after all, and didn't want us to bring a house in over his driveway at the other house either. I do still have a slightly less good option under evaluation, and a few other things I might try.
